OUR PROGRAMS

Our core educational offerings are designed to celebrate heritage, encourage excellence, and strengthen community.

1. Unique Activities

Cultural Workshops

Offer workshops that explore African American history, art, and music. These can include storytelling sessions, art projects inspired by African American artists, and music classes featuring influential genres like jazz and blues.

Heritage Celebrations

Organize events that celebrate significant cultural holidays and milestones, such as Black History Month, Juneteenth, and Kwanzaa. These events can include performances, guest speakers, and community gatherings.
