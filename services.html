OUR PROGRAMS

Our core educational offerings are designed to celebrate heritage, encourage excellence, and strengthen community.

1. Unique Activities

Cultural Workshops

Offer workshops that explore African American history, art, and music. These can include storytelling sessions, art projects inspired by African American artists, and music classes featuring influential genres like jazz and blues.

Heritage Celebrations

Organize events that celebrate significant cultural holidays and milestones, such as Black History Month, Juneteenth, and Kwanzaa. These events can include performances, guest speakers, and community gatherings.

2. Cultural Focus

Curriculum Integration

Ensure that your curriculum includes African American literature, historical figures, and contributions to various fields. This can be woven into subjects like history, literature, and social studies.

Cultural Exchanges

Facilitate exchanges with other cultural communities to promote understanding and appreciation of diverse heritages. This can include virtual pen pals or collaborative projects with other schools.

3. Community Impact

Community Service Projects

Engage students in community service projects that benefit their local area while teaching them about civic responsibility and leadership.

Parent & Community Involvement

Encourage parents and community members to participate in school activities, such as volunteering, mentoring, or leading workshops, to strengthen community ties.

4. Community Building & Connection

Online Community Platform

Launch an online community platform where students, parents, and educators can connect, share resources, and support each other. This space can host discussions, share educational materials, and celebrate achievements.
